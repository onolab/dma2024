How to get Hawaii Tokai International College (HTIC).
  1. By taxi from Waikiki to HTIC, it takes 25-50 minute around 90-100 USD.
  2. By bus from Waikiki to HTIC, it takes about one hour, 3 USD per a trip (see below).
From Ala Moana bus station (Kona St + Opp Kona Iki St), take bus Express C and get off at Kualakai Pkwy + Hoomohala Ave. Then, 10minutes walk to HTIC. GoogleMap For detailed information, see the following pdf files:
